A little bit about me.

My journey into tech started after college where I earned B.A.'s in Multimedia Communications and African & African American Studies. After searching for a career path that would offer remote work options and an intro into the tech world, I found Skillcrush and started learning Web Design. While at Skillcrush, I learned Wireframes,UI / UX, Photoshop, HTML & HTML5, CSS, FTP, & more! I can help clients develop user personas and create mood boards too. I now utilize my keen eye for detail and love of beautiful online content websites for clients who want to get a simple yet memorable place on the web.

I use my social media knowledge to work with those who want to improve their presence and build a strong community on social media platforms as a Social Media Strategist. As a Virtual Assistant, I love to help busy people who are hustling to grow their brand and want a helping hand to juggle life's sometimes mundane tasks like scheduling appointments and calling companies about those pesky bill errors! When I'm not working, I like to research my family's genealogy, binge watch my fave shows, listen to music (mostly Beyonce or Adele), & get lost in the rabbit hole that is Pinterest. :)
